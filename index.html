FALCON 9 OPERATION

Falcon 9, the first orbital class rocket capable of reflight designed and manufactured by SpaceX.

Falcon 9 made history in 2012 when it delivered the Dragon Spacecraft into the correct orbit for rendezvous with the International Space Station, making SpaceX the first commercial company ever to visit the station.

The simple two-stage configuration minimizes the number of separation events to safely complete its mission even in the event of an engine shutdown. The goal of SpaceX is to make rocket reusability the key breakthrough needed to reduce the cost of access to space and enable people to live on other planets.
